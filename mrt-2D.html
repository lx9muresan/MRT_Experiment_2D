Mental Rotation Test

Vandenberg & Kuse (1978) — Desktop Version

In each trial you will see a 3-D target object and four answer options. Exactly two of the four options show the same object rotated to a different orientation. The other two are mirror images that cannot be rotated to match.

You can click and drag the target object to rotate it freely in 3-D. The four answer options are shown at fixed orientations.

You will first complete a short practice block with feedback, then two timed test blocks of 10 trials each (5 minutes per block).

Instructions

Your task: For each trial, select the two answer options (out of four) that show the same shape as the target — just rotated. The other two are mirror-flipped and will never match no matter how you rotate them.

  • Rotate the target figure by clicking and dragging on it. The four answer options are fixed and cannot be rotated.
  • Click ↺ Reset on the target to snap it back to its starting orientation.
  • Press A B C D to toggle selection of each option.
  • Press Enter or click Submit once you have selected exactly two answers.
  • You cannot go back to a previous trial.

You will have 5 minutes for each block of 10 trials. Work as quickly and as accurately as you can. The practice block has no time limit and will show you whether your answers are correct.
